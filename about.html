Our mission: build real eco-friendly habits through play.

Why EcoQuest?

Theory alone doesn’t build habits. EcoQuest blends interactive learning, daily actions, and positive competition to help students turn knowledge into practice.

How it works

  • Learn via quizzes with explanations
  • Complete real-world challenges with photo verification
  • Earn Eco-Points, badges, and certificates
  • Track progress on leaderboards

For Schools

Schools get a simple way to run eco-programs, verify tasks, and motivate students with recognition and analytics.
